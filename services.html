WHAT WE DO

Comprehensive Infrastructure Solutions

Specialized engineering services for heavy foundations, bridges, and industrial projects.

Pile Foundation Work

As our core expertise, we specialize in high-capacity Bored Cast-in-Situ Piles designed to support massive structures in challenging soil conditions. Our fleet of hydraulic rotary rigs allows us to execute piling works with precision and speed, ensuring stability for bridges, metro lines, and high-rise buildings.

  • Diameter Range: 300mm to 1500mm piles.
  • Versatility: Execution in soft soil, mixed strata, and hard rock.
  • Driven Precast Piles: Installation of pre-moulded concrete piles for rapid foundation establishment.
  • Micro & Mini Piles: Solutions for restricted access areas and retrofitting.

Civil Construction & Infrastructure

We deliver end-to-end civil engineering services for large-scale transportation projects. Our team is experienced in executing complex works for Roads, Highways, and Railway Over Bridges (ROBs). We handle the entire lifecycle from ground engineering to structural completion, ensuring compliance with NHAI and Railway standards.

  • Highway Infrastructure: Heavy-duty foundations for flyovers and viaducts.
  • River Bridges: Specialized piling and pier construction in water bodies (e.g., Chambal & Ganga rivers).
  • Pile Caps & Rafts: Construction of massive reinforced concrete caps to distribute structural loads.

Drilling & Structural Works

Beyond foundations, we provide comprehensive structural solutions and deep drilling services. Our workshops are equipped to fabricate heavy steel components required for reinforcement cages and liners, ensuring high-quality material integration at the site.

  • Borewell Drilling: Deep drilling services for commercial, industrial, and infrastructure water requirements.
  • Heavy Steel Structures: Fabrication and erection of structural steel for industrial applications.
  • Steel Cage Making: On-site fabrication of high-tensile steel reinforcement cages for piling.
  • Hard Rock Drilling: Specialized TMR and Rotary drilling in difficult granite/rock strata.
