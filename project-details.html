Etawah-Kanpur NCR
Bypass Project

Project Overview

Construction of Road Over Bridges (ROBs) including approaches at 4 locations in lieu of level crossings on the CNB-ETW section in Prayagraj Division.

This project required high-precision hydraulic piling in challenging soil conditions. Shree Govind Construction was awarded the contract for boring, cage lowering, and concreting of piles.

Key Challenges

  • Strict timeline compliance for Railway safety blocks.
  • Deep piling requirement of 1200mm diameter.
  • Working in high-traffic zones near active railway tracks.

Our Solution

We deployed our XCMG 220 Hydraulic Rig to ensure rapid drilling. Our team worked 24/7 shifts to meet the "Traffic Block" windows provided by the railways, ensuring zero disruption to train services.
